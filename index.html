About Who We Are

Wedding Cinematography & Storytelling

Phones just don't feel this way. We believe in emotion, in timelessness, and in capturing moments that matter.

We are Keepsake Weddings, a team of passionate wedding content creators based in Galle, Sri Lanka — available worldwide. We specialize in capturing the moments beyond the posed and planned – the candid smiles, fleeting glances, and the authentic atmosphere>as it naturally unfolds. With nearly a decade of experience in live events and over five years focused on specialized content creation and social media, our deep understanding of storytelling is combined with technical mastery.

Our philosophy is simple: capture real emotion with elegance. We bring atimeless, cinematic portrayal to your wedding, authentically telling your story as it happens. Using vintage-styled cameras, we produce short-form videos and photographs that truly stand apart in both quality and immediacy. Every project is approached with care, creativity, and a deep respect for your unique narrative. Whether through stunning photographs, captivating reels, or comprehensive short films – we weave your memories into a single, living keepsake, delivered swiftly so your day remains fresh and vibrant. Let’s connect on WhatsApp and begin planning how your love story will be told.

Pricing Plans

Curated Keepsake Packages

The Glimpse

· Perfect for intimate ceremonies ·

Ideal for intimate ceremonies or civil weddings, this package focuses on capturing the essence of your special moments. You'll receive 20–30 edited vertical videos/photos (a mix of both), along with one 15–30 second teaser reel, all delivered via WhatsApp on the same day.

What's included: 1 content creator, up to 2 hours of coverage.
Starting at: LKR 35,000









The Keepsake Edit

· For a fuller narrative of your day ·

Designed for couples who desire a more comprehensive story of their day, this package expands on the candid captures. You'll get 50+ edited photos & videos, and two short-form reels (30–60 sec), delivered to you on the same day via Google Drive & WhatsApp.

What's included: 1 content creator, up to 5 hours of coverage.
Starting at: LKR 65,000









The Whole Story

· For full-day & multi-location weddings ·

This comprehensive package provides full-day coverage, perfect for multi-location weddings. You'll receive 100+ edited behind-the-scenes photos/videos,3–4 reels(with music + voiceovers optional), and a beautifulbehind-the-scenes montage edit (2–3 mins). All content is delivered on the same day with a follow-up album.

What's included:2 content creators, up to 8 hours of coverage.
Starting at:LKR 120,000
