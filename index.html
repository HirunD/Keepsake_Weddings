About Who we are

Wedding Cinematography & Storytelling

Phones just don't feel this way. We believe in emotion, in timelessness, and in capturing moments that matter.

We are Keepsake Weddings, a team of passionate wedding cinematographers based in Galle, Sri Lanka — available worldwide. We’re committed to crafting visual stories that are authentically yours. From every subtle glance to the loudest laugh, we preserve the magic of your day as it truly unfolds.

Our philosophy is simple: capture real emotion with elegance. Every project is approached with care, creativity, and a deep respect for your story. Whether by stills, reels, or full-length films — we weave memories into a single, living keepsake. Let’s connect on WhatsApp and begin planning how your love story will be told.

Services What we do

Wedding Cinematography & Photography

We don’t just capture images — we capture emotions, moments, and stories that last a lifetime. Our services are crafted to create timeless keepsakes of your wedding day, blending artistry with authenticity.
  • Cinematic Wedding Films

    Story-driven films that capture every emotion, laugh, and tear with cinematic style and heartfelt direction.

  • Fine Art Photography

    Elegant portraits and candid moments captured with an artistic eye to create stunning wedding day memories.

  • Photo & Video Packages

    Complete coverage with seamless coordination between photography and videography for a unified storytelling experience.

  • Destination Weddings

    Worldwide availability to capture your unique celebration, whether intimate or grand, wherever love takes you.
